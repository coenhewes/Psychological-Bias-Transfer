# Relevant Literature & Citations

## Core Reference: Emotional Manifold in Latent Space
**Title:** Emotions Where Art Thou: Understanding and Characterizing the Emotional Latent Space of Large Language Models
**Relevance:** This paper proves that large language models (LLMs) internally represent human emotions across a "low-dimensional emotional manifold" distributed across their hidden layers.
**How to use it:** Cite this to establish that the structural capacity for emotion exists in the weights. Their research proves the "bucket" exists in the latent space; our PBT research proves that this bucket can be permanently altered or "infected" via fine-tuning (Psychological Bias Transfer).
